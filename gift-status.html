What you can — and can't — see here

This page shows where the book stands: which stage it's in and what happens next. It never shows the stories themselves. While we work, your storyteller's answers stay between them and their coach — the finished book is theirs to share with you. That privacy is a promise we make to them, and it's part of why the stories come out true.

If your storyteller would like you to see a little more — the book's working title and how many chapters they've confirmed — they can turn on sharing from their own portal. It's off unless they choose it.

Questions, or want to add photos to the book? Just write to hello@coachlibra.org — include the email address you purchased with.
