Gentle answers

The questions families ask.

What if my parent isn't good with technology?

That's exactly who we built this for. They can answer by phone call, or one tap on a text message — no apps, no passwords, nothing to install. If they ever get stuck, a real person is a phone call away.

What if they don't finish?

They will. Getting a reluctant storyteller across the finish line is the single thing we're best at — gentle pacing, real encouragement, and a promise that a beautiful, complete book arrives no matter what. You will never end up with blank pages.

Whose words end up in the book?

Theirs. Always. We arrange and lightly polish their own stories so they read beautifully — but we never invent a memory or replace their voice with ours. This is a legacy of their words, not ours.

Can we make one for a parent who has passed?

Yes — tenderly. We can build a legacy book from their recordings, letters, and the memories of the people who loved them. Reach out and we'll guide your family through it with care.

How long does it take?

Most families finish in a few unhurried months of once-a-week storytelling. If you're working against a milestone or precious time, ask about our rapid keepsake — we can move quickly when it matters.

Is their voice really in the book?

Yes. Scan a page and you'll hear them tell that very story — their laugh, their pauses, their way of saying it. It's the closest thing to sitting beside them again.
